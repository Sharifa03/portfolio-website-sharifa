De lieve groene stad

For "de lieve groene stad" we were tasked with choosing an environmentally friendly initiative and making a web-based tour guide for people who already live in Amsterdam. We visited Vegan Junk Food Bar and decided to make our app about them. A nice restaurant located in De Pijp where you can enjoy plant-based foods, which help us all towards a better environment.

boeken zoeker amsterdam

This project focuses on improving reading accessibility for elementary school children by transforming the Boekenzoeker platform, developed by the City of Amsterdam. The goal is to redesign the platform with engaging and interactive elements that cater to a younger audience, making the experience of discovering books enjoyable and stimulating for these children.
